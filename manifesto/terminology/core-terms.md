# Mnemosyne — Core Terms

## AI Organizational Entropy

Operational fragmentation caused by insufficient continuity across human and AI cognition.

---

## Continuity Infrastructure

Systems designed to preserve causal and operational continuity across time.

---

## Governed Operational Memory

Human-reviewed persistence of organizational reasoning and causal lineage.

---

## Cognitive Flight Recorder

A continuity system capable of reconstructing operational cognition after failure, drift, or contradiction.

---

## Institutional Cognition

The evolving operational reasoning accumulated across humans, systems, workflows, and organizations.

---

## Continuity vs Retrieval

Retrieval finds information.

Continuity preserves evolving operational coherence.

---

## Human-Governed Persistence

The principle that humans remain responsible for validating and governing persistent organizational cognition.

---

## Causal Continuity

The preservation of reasoning lineage, assumptions, constraints, and operational evolution across time.

---

## Organizational Coherence

The ability of an organization to preserve aligned reasoning and operational continuity despite changing people, systems, workflows, and AI agents.
